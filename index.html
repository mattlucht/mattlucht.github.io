Matt Lucht

Hi, I'm Matt. I'm a delivery shaped person. I've worked in digital for many years, and remember the pre-internet times.

Places that I've worked:

NHS England

I'm currently working with NHS England looking at how we can improve the breast screening service.

Ministry of Housing, Community and Local Government

I've had a couple of spells working with MHCLG as a part of the Digital Planning team. I was initially a part of the discovery / alpha team who built Digital Land as a place to experiement and learn – there's lots of history in our weeknotes.

Digital Land evolved into planning.data.gov.uk where I led the data standards team.

Centre for Digital Public Services

Working with CDPS I was involved in 3 different projects:

  • supporting the Welsh Revenue Authority to become a fully digital tax organisation
  • with Natural Resource Wales we ran a digital lab called Learn By Making
  • meeting content design challenges in local government
HM Courts and Tribunal Services

At HMCTS I worked on the Apply for Probate service where I led 3 teams. One looking at how to apply for probate from a citizen perspective, one from the perspective of a probate professional, and also how the probate offices managed the applications.

BT Wholesale
